Introduction to Anyswap

Anyswap is a pioneering cross-chain decentralized exchange (DEX) and bridge protocol, enabling users to securely swap and bridge assets between a wide range of blockchains. Launched in 2020 and rebranded as Multichain, Anyswap remains a cornerstone of DeFi interoperability, supporting seamless movement of tokens and liquidity across leading networks like Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Fantom, Polygon, Avalanche, and more.

What is Anyswap?

Anyswap was created to address the fragmentation of liquidity and assets across multiple blockchains. It utilizes secure smart contracts, a network of distributed nodes (SMPC), and innovative router contracts to facilitate cross-chain swaps and bridging. Users can easily transfer tokens between chains with minimal fees, rapid confirmation times, and no custodial risk.

  • Supports 25+ blockchains including Ethereum, BNB Chain, Fantom, Polygon, Avalanche, Arbitrum, Optimism, and more
  • Fast, low-cost asset transfers across chains
  • Non-custodial security powered by audited smart contracts and SMPC nodes
  • Simple, intuitive user interface

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Use Anyswap

  1. Connect Your Wallet: Use MetaMask, WalletConnect, or other supported wallets.
  2. Select Chains: Choose the source and destination blockchains.
  3. Choose Asset: Select the token to bridge (e.g., ETH, USDT, etc.).
  4. Enter Amount: Specify the transfer amount and review estimated fees.
  5. Approve and Confirm: Approve the transaction in your wallet and sign to execute.
  6. Track Progress: Monitor transaction status in the Anyswap dashboard until completion.
